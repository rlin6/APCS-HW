Your Name



Your Name, or Kimi no Na Wa, is an anime movie that I've seen recently that I would highly recommend to you all. Even though it's pretty "weeb", it's really popular in the US theaters where it's staying for a while (so hurry and go see it! English subbed of course).





Pros

  • The handdrawn animation is BEAUTIFUL, see gif for reference.
  • The soundtrack is amazing and really adds onto the story
  • It has a very emotional storyline dealing with issues that some teens like me deal with. You might cry, you might not, but the story is still really unique and interesting.

Cons

  • Well it is anime so some people are really against it just because of that which is kinda sad.
  • The story can be confusing at first, romance a bit cheesy, and some plotholes but don't think too deep about the story and it should be good! Also, there's a lot of Eastern customs stuff that American viewers might not understand
  • It's not long enough!
