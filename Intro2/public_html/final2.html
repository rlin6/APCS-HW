About The Page

Oh hi there! Welcome to Pokemon Battle Simulator! To get started, please click on the Start Battle image on the home page. First, pick your 6 Pokemons from the 6 lists of the final forms of the Generation I Pokemon. Then, go ahead and pick each of the Pokemon's four moves from the list provided. Finally, you're on the best part! To battle, simply pick the move you wish to use and watch the enemy's HP go down! Have fun battling!

This website is a Computer Science Interactive Webpage created by Jason Kim and Ricky Lin for their CS Final Project. To play this game, it is recommended that you have some basic knowledge of the Pokemon World.
